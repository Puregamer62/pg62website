About Me

My name is Michael Sampino (aka Puregamer62). I am a beginner in software development. I have created a few apps (for IOS) and I plan to publish one of my apps this year (2018). I also have experience with C# (which I know better than swift). I started coding in 2015, when I made an RTE (real time edit) tool for JTAG and RGH Xbox 360 consoles. This tool was known at first as Puregamer62's XRPC Tool, but later became Puregamer62's Multi-Cod Tool when I made the tool paid. In 2016 I started to learn swift, but began high school which ended up taking away a lot of the time to code. Now I am continuing where I left off. This website is my next step to publishing my first app since a support website is required.
